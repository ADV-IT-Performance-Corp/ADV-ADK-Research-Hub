# McKinsey AI in Marketing Strategy

**Source**: McKinsey & Company  
**Focus**: Enterprise AI marketing transformation

## Strategic Framework

### 1. AI-Powered Personalization
- Next-best-action recommendations
- Hyper-personalized content
- Real-time engagement optimization

### 2. Predictive Analytics
- Churn prediction
- Customer lifetime value modeling
- Price optimization

### 3. Marketing Mix Modeling
- Budget allocation
- Channel effectiveness
- ROI measurement

## Implementation Roadmap

| Phase | Timeline | Key Deliverables |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| 1. Foundation | 1-2 months | Data integration, AI readiness |
| 2. Optimization | 2-4 months | Pilot campaigns, KPI framework |
| 3. Scaling | 4-6 months | Full automation, continuous learning |

## Success Metrics
- 30-50% improvement in marketing efficiency
- 20-40% increase in customer engagement
- 15-30% higher marketing ROI
